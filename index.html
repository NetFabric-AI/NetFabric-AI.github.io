NetFabric

The Go-To Place to Answer Any Question About Your Network

Our team of ETH Zurich PhDs and professors is developing a next-gen network observability platform that seamlessly connects all network data sources, giving you real-time answers to any network question.

By combining Large Language Models (LLMs) with cutting-edge mathematical models backed by a decade of academic research, our platform empowers network professionals to resolve issues faster, identify significant alerts, anticipate future problems, and unlock powerful AIOps.
